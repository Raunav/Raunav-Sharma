Monte Carlo Cost and Investment Analysis

Leveraging probabilistic modeling to guide construction cost estimation and strategic investment allocation.

Project Overview

This project explores the application of Monte Carlo simulations in two key domains: cost estimation for construction projects and strategic investment allocation. By simulating various outcomes under uncertainty, the project provides actionable insights for decision-making.

Key Outcomes

  • Construction Cost Estimation: Developed a probabilistic model to estimate costs, ensuring adequate contingency funds under uncertain conditions.
  • Investment Allocation: Created a simulation to evaluate risk-return trade-offs for portfolio decisions, optimizing allocation strategies over a 10-year horizon.

Tools Used

  • Macros
  • Conditional Formatting
