Coffee Shop Sales and Trends Analysis

Transforming sales data into actionable insights for business growth.

Project Overview

This project involved analyzing detailed sales data from a coffee shop to uncover trends, assess performance, and support decision-making. By leveraging descriptive analytics techniques, the project aimed to identify the best-performing items, sales patterns, and areas for operational improvement. The insights were designed to guide strategies for enhancing profitability and customer satisfaction.

Key Outcomes

  • Identified the top-selling products and peak sales periods, enabling targeted promotions and inventory optimization.
  • Analyzed customer preferences to support personalized marketing campaigns.
  • Delivered actionable insights on underperforming items, leading to data-driven recommendations for menu adjustments.

Tools Used

  • Microsoft Excel
  • Pivot Tables
  • Charts
  • Descriptive Statistics
