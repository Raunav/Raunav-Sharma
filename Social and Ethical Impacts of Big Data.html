Social and Ethical Impacts of Big Data

Exploring the balance between innovation and responsibility in data systems.

Project Overview

This project delves into the profound societal and ethical implications of big data systems, focusing on their ability to transform lives while also posing challenges like inequality and privacy concerns. The analysis uses real-world systems like Aadhaar as a case study.

Key Outcomes

  • Understanding the dichotomy between "low-resolution" and "high-resolution" citizens.
  • Exploring the ethical implications of data collection and usage.
  • Highlighting the role of big data in public policy and social equity.

Applications

Big data systems have the potential to revolutionize public administration, improve societal welfare, and drive business innovation. Ethical frameworks and robust regulations are crucial for their responsible application.

Tools and Frameworks

  • Case Studies (e.g., Aadhaar System)
  • Articles and Research Papers
  • Ethical and Social Frameworks

Future Goals

I aspire to harness the power of big data to create equitable solutions in education, healthcare, and public policy, ensuring data ethics remain at the forefront of innovation.
