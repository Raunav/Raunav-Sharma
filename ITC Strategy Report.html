ITC Ltd. Strategy Analysis

A comprehensive strategic report analyzing ITC Ltd.'s competitive positioning and growth strategies.

Project Overview

This individual strategic report critically evaluates ITC Ltd., a diversified conglomerate operating in FMCG, agriculture, hospitality, and paper industries. The project examines ITC's competitive positioning using strategic tools and frameworks.

Strategic Frameworks

  • Porter’s Five Forces: Analysis of industry competition, supplier power, buyer power, threats of new entrants, and substitute products.
  • STEEP Analysis: A deep dive into Social, Technological, Economic, Environmental, and Political factors influencing ITC.
  • SWOT Analysis: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats specific to ITC Ltd.

Key Insights

  • ITC's dominance in the FMCG sector is driven by its diversification strategies.
  • Sustainability initiatives align with environmental goals, enhancing ITC's competitive advantage.
  • Opportunities exist in expanding the digital and e-commerce segments.

Tools Used

  • Porter’s Five Forces
  • STEEP Analysis
  • SWOT Analysis
  • Microsoft Excel (Data Visualization)
