The Ethical Implications of AI

Exploring innovation, responsibility, and the balance of ethics in AI development.

Project Overview

This project delves into the profound impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies on modern society. From transforming industries to redefining daily life, AI-powered systems have reshaped human interactions with technology. The project focuses on evaluating the ethical and societal implications of rapid AI advancements, including privacy, bias, transparency, and accountability. Using a multidisciplinary approach, the project aims to address pressing questions about the responsible development and deployment of AI.

Key Outcomes

  • Investigated state-of-the-art AI tools, such as ChatGPT, Bard, and IBM Watson, analyzing their capabilities and limitations in handling large-scale data and performing complex tasks.
  • Examined critical issues such as biased algorithms, privacy concerns, and the risks of unregulated AI deployment.
  • Proposed frameworks for achieving a balance between innovation and regulation, emphasizing the importance of ethical oversight and global collaboration.

Tools Used

  • Research Literature Analysis
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) Models
  • Big Data Analytics
